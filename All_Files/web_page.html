Strategic Planning

Strategic planning is a crucial organizational process where leaders define a vision for the future, set goals and objectives, and establish a roadmap for achieving them. It involves analyzing the current situation, anticipating future challenges, and making informed decisions about resource allocation to ensure the organization stays aligned with its mission and vision. The senior leadership of an organization is generally tasked with determining strategy. It is executed by strategic planners or strategists, who involve many parties and research sources in their analysis of the organization and its relationship to the environment in which it competes.
